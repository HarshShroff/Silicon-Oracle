Overfitting Warning

Past performance is not indicative of future results. Use backtesting to validate trading logic and understand strategy behavior, not to predict future returns.

  • • Don't Overfit: Avoid tweaking parameters until you get perfect results
  • • Market Changes: Conditions that worked in the past may not repeat
  • • Use for Learning: Understand how strategies behave in different market conditions
  • • Walk Forward: Test on out-of-sample data to validate robustness
